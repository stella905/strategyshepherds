The Process

One session. A full month of content.

Both programs follow the same simple process. You show up once a month for a recorded conversation. Our team turns that conversation into a full month of authority-building content.

01

Clarify — Month One Only

We start with a kickoff positioning session to get clear on what you want to be known for, who you are speaking to, and what your authority should stand for. This happens once and sets the foundation for everything that follows.

02

Record — Every Month

Each month, you sit down with us for one recorded session. One conversation. That is all we need from you. We come prepared with questions, prompts, and a structure that pulls out your best thinking.

03

We Produce Everything

Our team takes your recording and turns it into a full month of content: a long-form video, short-form videos, blog posts, and LinkedIn posts — all written and edited in your voice, and optimised for Social SEO so your ideas rank on Google and AI search.

04

You Publish and Grow

You receive everything ready to publish. You post, your audience grows, and your authority compounds. The longer you show up consistently, the stronger your influence becomes.
