The Authority Challenge

A four-week live cohort for business leaders, executives, and senior professionals who are ready to build their authority online — with clarity, structure, and the right AI tools to make it sustainable.

This is not a course. Stella works directly with each participant. Over four weeks, the cohort moves from diagnosis to a complete, AI-powered 90-day authority plan — ready to implement on the day the sprint ends. Seats are limited and accepted by application, so every participant is someone we are confident can achieve the outcome.

"AI is your authority amplifier, not your replacement. The leaders who figure that out first will define the next decade of professional influence."

Week 1

Authority Audit and Clarity

Where do you stand? What are you uniquely qualified to say? Define your authority position.

Week 2

Building the Content Architecture

Turn your position into a structured content system. Topics, formats, channels, and cadence.

Week 3

AI Tools and Automation

The specific AI workflows that make your authority system sustainable. Work smarter, publish consistently.

Week 4

The 90-Day Authority Plan

Your complete implementation plan: what to publish, where, when, and how to measure traction.
